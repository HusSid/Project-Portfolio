Automated Buggy

A buggy was tasked with navigating itself to the end location by scanning coloured cards and moving accordingly based on their colour.

The PIC microcontroller was programmed to process colour sensor data and detect the colour of each card so the buggy could move appropriately by supplying the correct level of power to the relevant motors.

The buggy was able to reach the final card in the GIF above but performance varied depending on:
  • Ambient lighting conditions.
  • Surface texture.
  • Battery levels.

Including a calibration stage could account for these variations and produce more consistent results.
